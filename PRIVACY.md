# Privacy Policy

**Job Finder — Privacy Policy**
*Last updated: March 2026*

---

**The short version:** Job Finder stores everything locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. We have no access to your data.

---

## 1. What We Collect

Job Finder collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Job listings captured from third-party platforms
- Your search criteria and profile preferences
- Your application history and status notes
- Scores and evaluation results generated by the scoring engine
- Source configuration (platform URLs, search parameters)

None of this data is transmitted to Job Finder, its contributors, or any third party.

## 2. What We Do Not Collect

- Your name, email address, or identity
- Your credentials or login information for any platform
- Browsing history outside of Job Finder-initiated captures
- Analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry of any kind

## 3. Local Storage and Retention

All data is stored in a SQLite database on your local machine at `data/jobs.db`. Job Finder has no access to your data and cannot modify or delete it on your behalf.

Job Finder provides in-app controls for managing your local data, including manual deletion and optional automatic deletion schedules. **You are solely responsible for configuring, monitoring, and using these settings.** Job Finder makes no guarantee that automatic deletion will occur if your device is offline, the software is not running, or settings are misconfigured.

Uninstalling Job Finder does not automatically delete your stored data. To ensure complete removal, use the in-app data management tools before uninstalling, or manually delete the `data/` directory from your installation folder.

## 4. Third-Party Platforms

When you use Job Finder to capture data from LinkedIn, Indeed, or other platforms, you are interacting with those platforms directly through your browser. Job Finder does not intercept, store, or transmit your credentials. Those platforms' own privacy policies govern your relationship with them, including how they collect and use data about your browsing activity.

## 5. Optional Telemetry

Job Finder does not currently collect any telemetry or usage analytics. If a future version introduces optional telemetry, it will be clearly disclosed, off by default, and require explicit opt-in before any data is transmitted.

## 6. Jurisdiction-Specific Rights

**California residents (CCPA):** Job Finder does not sell personal information. Because all data is stored locally on your device, Job Finder does not hold personal information subject to CCPA access or deletion requests. You control your data directly.

**EEA and UK residents (GDPR):** Job Finder processes no personal data on its own systems. All data processing occurs locally on your device under your sole control. Job Finder is not a data controller or processor with respect to your personal data under GDPR. No cross-border data transfers occur because no data leaves your device.

## 7. Security

Because all data is stored locally, the security of your Job Finder data depends on the security of your device. We recommend standard device security practices (disk encryption, strong passwords, screen lock). Job Finder does not provide additional encryption of the local database beyond your operating system's protections.

## 8. Children

Job Finder is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Because we collect no data at all, this is largely a precautionary statement.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be noted in the software changelog and surfaced at next launch for material changes.

## 10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on the Job Finder GitHub repository.
