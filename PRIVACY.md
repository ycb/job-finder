# Privacy Policy

**Job Finder — Privacy Policy**
*Last updated: March 2026*

---

**The short version:** Job Finder stores your job-search data locally on your device. Optional product telemetry is off by default and only sent when you explicitly enable it.

---

## 1. What We Collect Locally

Job Finder stores the following data **locally on your device**:

- Job listings captured from third-party platforms
- Your search criteria and profile preferences
- Your application history and status notes
- Scores and evaluation results generated by the scoring engine
- Source configuration (platform URLs, search parameters)
- Onboarding state, including install channel and consent choices

## 2. What We Do Not Collect By Default

By default, Job Finder does not transmit your local job-search data to maintainers or contributors.

Job Finder does not intentionally collect:

- your passwords or authentication tokens,
- your full browser history outside Job Finder-initiated capture actions,
- or payment data.

## 3. Local Storage and Retention

All local data is stored on your machine (for example, SQLite at `data/jobs.db` and local config/state files). Job Finder maintainers do not have direct access to this data.

Job Finder provides controls for managing local data, including manual deletion and optional automatic deletion schedules. **You are responsible for configuring and reviewing these settings.**

Uninstalling Job Finder does not automatically delete your stored data. To remove local state fully, use in-app data management or delete the `data/` directory manually.

## 4. Third-Party Platforms

When you capture from LinkedIn, Indeed, Wellfound, or other platforms, you interact with those services through your browser session. Their own privacy policies govern those interactions.

## 5. Optional Telemetry (Consent-Based)

Job Finder can send limited product-quality telemetry **only when you enable anonymous metrics**.

When enabled, telemetry may include:

- install/channel metadata,
- feature usage events,
- high-level run outcomes and counts,
- non-sensitive diagnostics.

Telemetry is intended to improve product quality and onboarding. You can disable metrics later in settings. If disabled, telemetry events are not sent.

If you configure external integrations (for example Supabase), data sent to those services is governed by their terms and privacy policies.

## 6. Jurisdiction-Specific Rights

**California residents (CCPA):** Job Finder does not sell personal information. Primary job-search records remain local to your device unless you explicitly enable telemetry or external integrations.

**EEA/UK residents (GDPR):** Primary job-search records are processed locally on your device. Any optional telemetry you enable is processed according to your explicit settings and the configured service providers.

## 7. Security

Because data is stored locally by default, your data security depends on your device security. Use standard protections such as disk encryption, strong passwords, and screen locks.

## 8. Children

Job Finder is not intended for users under 13.

## 9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy over time. Material changes are reflected in the changelog and surfaced in-product.

## 10. Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue on the Job Finder GitHub repository.
